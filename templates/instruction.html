Instructions to the candidate.

   Students are requested to strictly adhere to the following rules. Failing so will result in malpractice and result will be withheld
  1. Candidates must have their id card.
  2. No one other than the candidate should be present in the room.
  3. The candidate should be in a well lit room without any background noise.
  4. The face should be clearly visible.
  5. Usage of mobile phones or any other electronic gadgets during the exam is strictly prohibited.
  6. Do not divert the focus from the screen. Eye movements and physical posture will be monitored and any suspicious activity will be treated as malpractice.
  7. A total of 3 warnings will be given to the candidate. The same will be displayed. If the count exceeds 3 then the session will be terminated.
  8. The microphone and camera should remain on during the entire duration of the exam.
  9. Do not try to communicate to anyone during the exam. Such activities will be recorded.
  10. The candidate should remain online till the end of the exam.
